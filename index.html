üìñ About PK Web Tools

Our Mission

PK Web Tools was created with a singular vision: to provide powerful, professional-grade productivity tools that respect your privacy and data sovereignty. In an era where most online services harvest user data for profit, we stand firmly against this practice. Our mission is to demonstrate that exceptional web applications can be built without compromising user privacy. Every tool in our suite runs entirely within your browser, utilizing modern web technologies to deliver desktop-class functionality without requiring server-side processing or data transmission. We believe that your documents, notes, and personal information should remain yours alone.

Who We Serve

Our tools are designed for students, professionals, freelancers, and anyone who values both productivity and privacy. Whether you're a student managing assignments and studying from previous year questions, a developer organizing project milestones, a writer drafting content in our scratchpad, or a professional working with sensitive PDF documents, PK Web Tools provides the functionality you need. We've carefully crafted each tool to be intuitive enough for beginners while offering the depth that power users demand. Our mobile-responsive design ensures you can be productive whether you're at your desk or on the go, and our lightweight architecture means these tools work even on older devices or slow connections.

Privacy Philosophy

Privacy isn't just a feature‚Äîit's the foundation of everything we build. Unlike traditional web applications that process your data on remote servers, every tool in the PK Web Tools suite operates using client-side JavaScript, meaning all processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device, and we don't use cookies, analytics trackers, or any technology that monitors your behavior. We don't collect emails, we don't require accounts, and we certainly never sell data because we don't have any data to sell. This is privacy by design, not privacy as an afterthought. Our open-source codebase means anyone can audit our privacy claims, and our commitment to remaining ad-free on tools with substantial content ensures that user experience is never compromised for monetization.
