Personal Growth Plan (Summary)

1. Daily Structure (Exam Focused)

Wake up at a fixed time.

2 hours of focused study in the morning (core subjects + upcoming practicals).

Afternoon: revise practical files or procedures for 1 hour.

Evening: 1.5 hours of theory preparation (priority subjects first).

Keep phone away during study sessions.


2. Strengthen Academic Basics

Study first-year core subjects for 1 hour daily.

Focus on understanding concepts clearly.


3. Communication Improvement

Read aloud for 10 minutes daily.

Record yourself once a week for self-review.


4. Learn One Marketable Skill

Choose AutoCAD or Data Analysis.

Practice 30 minutes daily.


5. C and C++ Learning Plan

Start with C for fundamentals.

Dedicate 30 minutes daily to coding.

After mastering basics of C, move to C++.

Use one consistent resource and practice by writing code.


6. Reduce Mindless Scrolling

Use app blockers on distracting apps.

Allocate specific times for social media.


7. Social Interaction Practice

Engage in small conversations with peers.

Improve confidence by talking regularly.


8. Physical Activity

Walk, workout, or perform any activity for 20-30 minutes.


9. Weekly Review

Spend 10 minutes every Sunday reviewing what you completed.

Adjust next week's plan based on progress.


10. Exam Preparation Priorities

First priority: Practical exams (revise procedures, diagrams, formulas daily).

Second priority: Weak theory subjects. Study them every morning.

Third priority: Strong subjects. Revise them lightly in the evenings.

Solve previous year questions every 2-3 days.

No new long-term skills until exams end.
