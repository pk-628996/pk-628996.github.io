‚ùì FAQ

Q: How many tasks can I add?
A: There's no set limit, but browser storage is finite. The app can handle hundreds of tasks without issues.

Q: Will my tasks be saved if I close the browser?
A: Yes! Tasks are stored in your browser's local storage and persist between sessions.

Q: Can I access my tasks from different devices?
A: Tasks are stored locally per browser. Each device/browser will have its own separate task list.

Q: What happens if I clear my browser data?
A: Clearing browser data will delete your saved tasks. Consider exporting important tasks before clearing data.
